Username Rules
  • Cannot have any spaces.
  • Recipient name can only have maximum of 64 characters.
  • Recipient name cannot start with a special character.
  • Recipient name cannot end with a special character.
  • Recipient name must have all printable characters.
  • Recipient name cannot have two special characters together.
Password Rules
  • Must be between 8 and 30 characters.
  • Must have at least one upper case letter.
  • Must have at least one lower case letter.
  • Must have at least one number.
  • Must have at least one special character.
  • Cannot have any spaces.
  • Cannot contain username.
FEIN Rules
  • Must be 9 numbers.
  • Must have a dash after 2nd number.
  • Cannot have any spaces.
Email Rules
  • Cannot have any spaces.
  • Recipient name can only have maximum of 64 characters.
  • Recipient name cannot start with a special character.
  • Recipient name cannot end with a special character.
  • Recipient name must have all printable characters.
  • Recipient name cannot have two special characters together.
  • Domains must have an inner domain and a main domain, separated by a dot.
  • Inner domains can only have a maximum of 253 characters.
  • Inner domains can only have a dash or dot for special characters.
